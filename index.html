Robot & sense of touch

My master thesis at TUM was about mimicking the human sense of touch for robots. To be more precise, to compute the optimal speed which delivers the most information about the textures. The main application would be a full automation in quality controls.
The setup consists of three acceleremoters monted on a 6-axis robotic arm.

Comparison between Wavelet and Fourier Transform

The aim of this project was to compare how much information convey a Haar Wavelet and short Fourier transforms. The main goal of this comparison was to improve the texture detection.
